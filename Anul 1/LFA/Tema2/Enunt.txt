------------- TRANSFORMARE NFA TO DFA -------------------

INPUT: un NFA oarecare, dat prin stari, tranzitii, stari finale

OUTPUT: DFA-ul rezultat in urma aplicarii algoritmului de conversie asupra 
	NFA-ului primit ca input


------------- NFA TO DFA ( ENGLISH ) --------------------

INPUT: an ordinary NFA, given by its states and transitions;

OUTPUT: the DFA that resulted after the implementation of the conversion 
	algorithm applied to the given NFA.
